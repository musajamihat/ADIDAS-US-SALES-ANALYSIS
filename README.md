# ADIDAS USA SALES ANALYTICS DASHBOARD
## Project Review
This project is a **Power BI- based Analytics Dashboard** that provides a comprehensive overview of sales, profit, retailers, products and marketing trends.
This dashboard is designed to help stakeholders to monitor performance and derived insights across cities, states and regions

---

## Objectives
- Analyze sales performance across products and locations
- Tracks sales and profits trends to identify high performing areas
- Monitor Retailer performance and identify top-selling product over time
- Identify best selling retailer, product and sales methods

---

## Key Metrics (KPIs)
- **Total Sales:** $899,9M
-  **Profit:** $322,1M
-  **Total Quantity:** $2,478,861
-  **AVG sales:** $363.03

--- 

## Data Cleaning and Transformation
All data preparation was performed using **Power Query in Power BI**, including: 
- Remove du[plicate and handle missing values
- Standardize column names and data type
- Creating calculate columns
- Transforming raw data into analyzing-ready tables

---

## Data Analysis
Analysis was carried out using **DAX** enabling:
- Sales distribution across Sales methods
- Monthly sales treand analysis
- Total quantity, sales and profits distribtion by products
- Sales distribution by Retailers

---

## Data Visualization
Insights are visualized using **Build Visual Pane**
- Column chart for Top Retailers
- Line chart for mothly sales trends
- Donut chart for Sale method distribution
- Table chart showing product with the highest quantity sold, best selling product and profitable product

 ---

## Data Description 
The datasets includes:
- Product category
- Sales Channel
- Retailers
- Total sales, profits and quantity

---

## Key Insights
- Sales declined between Febuary and March and peaked between April and May showing progress. Sales dropped again in June and peaked in July,
  A gradual decline occurred from August to Octorber, followed by a peak between November and December.
- In-store sales generate more revenue compared to other sales method showing the most constitent and high-performing sales method. Stakeholder need to focus more
  on strenthening In-store strategies to maximize overall sales performance
- Sales performances in 2021 showed noticeable improvement compared to 2020 indicating over all business growth
- West region generate more revenue and profit compared to other regions, making it the highest-performing region overall
- Men's Street footwear outperform all other products in revenue and profit, indicating a strong customer demand and market preference
- Charleston and New York outperform all other cities in revenue and profit, making it the highest-performing cities overall
- New York, Texas, Califonia and Florida are top 4 highest-performing states, making them the leading states overall
